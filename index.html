About Me

Hello! My name is Michael Duong. I'm a board gamer, puzzle lover, problem-solver and a passionate front-end web developer.

After graduating from UC Berkeley in 2012 with a BA Philosophy, I've worked as a property manager, tutor, LSAT Logic Games question writer, tech support, and B2B salesman. Throughout it all, my brain always seemed to crave a more technical challenge. I found myself spending my spare time optimizing medieval subsistence farms in Agricola or devising programmatic ways to automate chemical manufacturing in SpaceChem, and I eventually took the hint. After working through online courses like Codecademy and FreeCodeCamp, I took the leap by quitting my job and enrolling in the LearningFuze 12-Week Full Immersion Accelerated Web Development Course, where I found exactly the kind of challenge that I craved.
I'm excited to see what kinds of new opportunities and challenges await me now, and I'd love to hear from you!
